Introduction to Vertical Slides

  • Use your down arrow button on your keyboard!
  • Click the down arrow on the bottom right hand side of this presentation!

Why Are Vertical Slides Useful?

  • Expand more on a single topic without having to crame everything on one slide!
  • Make your presentation look cleaner!
  • Make your presentation flow easier!

Statement of Problem

  • Have too much information to store on one slide?
  • Huge topics to cover?
  • Need easier transistions that keep your users engaged?
  • Wanting an organized style to keep topics grouped?

Use vertical slides!

  • They are good for mulitple slides of information on one topic!
  • Helpful on expanding into more depth on a certain topic!
  • Modern look on presenting keeping users engaged and wowed!

Challenges of vertical slides

  • Keeping things organized!
  • Not having enough information to create vertical slides for!
  • The transistions from slide to slide!
